01 — About

From raw events to trusted tables

Sricharan is a Data Analytics Engineer with 2.6+ years of experience designing end-to-end ETL pipelines and normalized data models across transactional, CRM, and call audit domains.

He builds multi-layer Bronze, Silver, Gold architectures on Delta Lake using PySpark, AWS EMR, and Glue — with the Gold layer serving BI teams through BigQuery and ML teams directly through Delta Lake. Hands-on with Kinesis, Firehose, Lambda, MWAA Airflow, and Databricks.

BRONZE — raw ingest
SILVER — cleansed, conformed
GOLD — single source of truth

One Gold layer, two consumers: BigQuery for BI reporting, Delta Lake for ML — no duplicate prep across teams.
